Ahmad's Book Recommendations

Book 1: The Great Gatsby by F. Scott Fitzgerald

A beautiful exploration of the American Dream, Gatsby's tragic pursuit of love and success is captivating and deeply moving.

Book 2: To Kill a Mockingbird by Harper Lee

A powerful novel on racism and justice in the American South, this book remains as relevant today as ever. The characters are unforgettable, especially Atticus Finch.

Book 3: 1984 by George Orwell

A haunting look at a dystopian future where totalitarianism reigns supreme. Orwell's vision is a chilling reminder of the dangers of unchecked power.
